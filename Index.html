Welcome to Candy Land: Rocky Road

The Rules

  1. Movement is decided by dice rolls
  2. Press the green button to roll dice
  3. Each player controls one die
  4. Licorice Lizard spaces will make you lose a turn
  5. First player to reach the end wins!

Locations

  1. The first part of the journey is Licorice Lane, where the Licorice Lizards came from
  2. Next up is the Marshmallow Meadows, home to the Gummy Grizzly
  3. Third is the Gram Canyon, where the Cinnamon Snails roam free
  4. The final part of the journey is the City of Chocolate, where chemistry teacher Walter White-Chocoltae lives
